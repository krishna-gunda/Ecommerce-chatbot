RETURN POLICY

General Return Window
Most items purchased from our store can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and include all accessories and manuals.

Electronics Return Window
Electronics such as laptops, mobile phones, and headphones have a shorter return window of 10 days from the date of delivery, due to rapid depreciation and testing requirements. Opened software licenses and activated SIM cards are not eligible for return.

Clothing and Footwear
Clothing and footwear can be returned within 15 days as long as tags are still attached and the item shows no signs of wear.

Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances: perishable goods, gift cards, personal care items once opened, and made-to-order or customized products.

How to Initiate a Return
Customers can initiate a return by providing their order ID through the support assistant or the "My Orders" page. Once initiated, a return pickup is scheduled within 2 to 4 business days.

Return Pickup and Packaging
Customers must pack the item securely in its original box. Our courier partner will collect the item from the registered delivery address. No return label printing is required for pickup returns.

Condition Assessment
All returned items go through a quality check upon arrival at our warehouse. If the item does not meet return conditions (missing parts, visible damage, signs of use), the return may be rejected and the item shipped back to the customer.
