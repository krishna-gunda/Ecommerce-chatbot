REFUND POLICY

Refund Timeline
Once a returned item passes quality inspection at our warehouse, refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days. The amount is credited to the original payment method used during purchase.

Refund Methods
If the original payment was made via credit card or debit card, the refund is credited back to the same card. If the original payment was made via UPI or net banking, the refund is credited to the same bank account. Cash on delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer, and customers must provide their bank account details after the return is approved.

Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if the returned item is missing accessories, has minor damage not caused during shipping, or if only part of a multi-item order is returned.

Refund for Cancelled Orders
If an order is cancelled before it is shipped, the full amount is refunded within 3 to 5 business days automatically, with no return process required.

Delayed Refunds
If a refund is not received within the stated timeline, customers should first check with their bank, since bank processing can take an additional 2 to 3 business days. If the delay persists beyond 10 business days from return approval, customers should contact support with their order ID for manual investigation.

Store Credit Option
Customers can optionally choose to receive their refund as store credit instead of a refund to the original payment method. Store credit refunds are processed instantly and come with a 5 percent bonus value.
