SHIPPING POLICY

Standard Delivery
Standard delivery takes 5 to 7 business days depending on the destination pin code. Orders are processed within 24 hours of being placed, then handed to our courier partner.

Express Delivery
Express delivery is available for select pin codes and delivers within 1 to 2 business days for an additional fee. Express orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day.

Shipping Charges
Orders above 499 rupees qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below this amount are charged a flat shipping fee of 49 rupees. Express delivery has a separate fee shown at checkout.

Order Tracking
Once an order is shipped, customers receive a tracking ID via email and SMS. Order status moves through the following stages: Placed, Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered. Customers can check their live order status anytime through the support assistant by providing their order ID.

Delayed Shipments
Shipments may be delayed due to weather conditions, regional holidays, or courier network congestion. If a shipment is delayed beyond 3 days past the expected delivery date, customers are eligible to request a shipping fee refund.

Failed Delivery Attempts
Our courier partner attempts delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts fail due to customer unavailability or an incorrect address, the order is returned to the origin warehouse and the customer is notified to reschedule delivery or request a refund.

International Shipping
We currently ship only within India. International shipping is not supported at this time.
